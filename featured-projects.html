Customer & Order Analysis

Performed a thorough analysis of customer and order data from various tables in a database. It includes queries to track January and February sales, counting iPhone purchases, identifying specific products and their revenues, analyzing sales in different locations, determining customer behavior regarding multiple product purchases, and investigating high-revenue products. These queries provide detailed insights into sales trends, customer buying patterns, and top-performing products within the dataset.

Spotify Analysis

Obtained a Spotify dataset from Kaggle and utilized SQLiteStudio to create a table for Spotify song data and ran queries to analyze aspects like average danceability per artist and track, identify top artists by popularity, find the longest song's artist, calculate danceability for top songs, list energetic song artists, and compute averages for popularity, danceability, and energy per artist and track. These queries offer insights into song attributes, artist performance, and popular tracks within the Spotify dataset.
